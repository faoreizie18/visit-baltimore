Top Attractions in Baltimore

The National Aquarium

Underwater journey through diverse marine life displays, including a notable jellyfish exhibit and interactive touch pool.

The Inner Harbor

Waterfront promenade featuring paddle boats and live music, with panoramic views from Federal Hill.

The Walters Art Museum

Art museum in Mount Vernon showcasing a broad array of global art across ages, highlighted by Greco-Roman and East Asian pieces.
